Hola!

Thea Stilton was born in New Mouse City, Mouse Island. She is a special correspondent for The Rodent's Gazette, Mouse Island's most famouse newspaper, which is edited by her famouse brother, Geronimo Stilton. A graduate of the prestigious Mouseford Academy, Ms. Stilton is a visiting professor of journalism at Mouseford today. She is also the author of numerous mystery novels based on the exciting lives of her friends and former students, the Thea Sisters. In her spare time, Ms. Stilton loves to travel and go on fabumouse adventures.
Geronimo's younger sister and the special correspondent for The Rodent's Gazette. For a time, Thea taught a journalism class at Whale Island University, and while there she met five mouselets. These mouselets, Pamela, Paulina, Violet, Colette, and Nicky named themselves the Thea sisters, after their favorite teacher, Thea Stilton. Thea loves to have an adventure with Trap, Benjamin, and Geronimo and exploring for an article in The Rodent's Gazette. Thea is always ready to jump into action. She also drives a motorcycle and a Helicopter which is portrayed to the book. While she is portrayed as brave and adventurous (and well prepared too), she is often reckless and often gets into trouble while searching for stories.
